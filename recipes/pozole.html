Pozole

One of the most delicious dishes from Mexico

This is a common dish that is cooked frequently in Mexico, normally we would prepare it when there's a special celebration such as 16 september (Mexico's Independence Day)

Ingredients

    For the soup:

  • 4 quarts of water
  • 2 pounds cubed pork shoulder
  • 1 pound pork spare ribs or baby back ribs
  • 1 white onion cut in quarts
  • 8 large garlic cloves
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 cans (15 ounces each) white hominy, drained and rinsed.
